üîç Trip Initiation Analysis: Unilateral vs Combined

Q: "Who usually initiates trip plans in your group, and how?"

  • 68.4% UNILATERAL (Single Person Initiates) - Most trips driven by individual leadership
  • 21.1% COMBINED (Group Collaborative) - Shared planning and split responsibilities

Key Insight: Clear preference for individual leadership over group collaboration. Tools should empower single initiators to coordinate groups effectively rather than assuming equal participation.
